How do I get an apostille in Colorado?
An apostille authenticates a document for use in another country. In Colorado, this is done through the Colorado Secretary of State's office. Aimee Skillin is a Certified Apostille Agent who handles the complete process: preparing your documents, notarizing them if needed, submitting everything to the Secretary of State, and keeping you updated every step of the way. She can even manage the process while you're abroad.
